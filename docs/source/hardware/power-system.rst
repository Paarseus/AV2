============
Power System
============

Dual-battery system: main battery for propulsion, auxiliary 12V battery for computing/sensors.

Auxiliary Battery (12V)
=======================

Powers all computing and sensor systems.

+-------------------+---------------------------+
| Type              | AGM or Lithium-ion        |
+-------------------+---------------------------+
| Voltage           | 12V nominal               |
+-------------------+---------------------------+
| Capacity          | 20-50 Ah                  |
+-------------------+---------------------------+

=================

| Component             | Typical  | Peak     |
+=======================+==========+==========+
| Main Computer         | 6 A      | 12 A     |
+-----------------------+----------+----------+
| Velodyne VLP-16       | 0.7 A    | 1 A      |
+-----------------------+----------+----------+
| Xsens MTi-630         | 0.2 A    | 0.3 A    |
+-----------------------+----------+----------+
| RealSense D435        | 0.7 A    | 1 A      |
+-----------------------+----------+----------+
| Teensy + CAN (Ã—4)     | 0.6 A    | 1.2 A    |
+-----------------------+----------+----------+
| **Total**             | ~10 A    | ~18 A    |
+-----------------------+----------+----------+

**Runtime**: ~5 hours with 50 Ah battery
